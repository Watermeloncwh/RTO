Course details:

Aopeng Education has built a network service system consisting of headquarters and learning centers, covering urban and rural areas and communities all over the country. At present, more than 1,900 cooperative learning centers have been set up all over the country, and a standardized and professional service team has been built. In cooperation with key universities, open universities and higher vocational colleges at home and abroad, flexible, convenient and personalized technical means have been used to provide support services in continuing education, open education and vocational skills training for scholars of different ages and occupations. Continuing education in colleges and universities. Cooperate with colleges and universities to carry out continuing education business, provide digital overall solutions for schools, and provide learners with one-stop support services from registration, study, counseling, practical training, examination and other links. Open education. Cooperate with open universities at all levels to provide online teaching support services; Provide platform, system and network operation and maintenance services; Provide special construction services for online courses (cloud training) resources, and help the Open University to use the new generation of information technology to carry out education and teaching. Vocational skills training. Platforms such as "massive open online course Net" and "OPEN2U" were selected into the "internet plus Vocational Skills Training Program" of the Ministry of Human Resources and Social Security and the Ministry of Finance, and were approved as national high-quality online vocational skills training platforms. More than 3,000 high-quality training courses were built, providing new technology vocational skills training services for more than 21 million IT employees. As the first recommended unit to obtain the qualification of "National Training Program" teacher training institution, it serves the professional development of teachers, and the annual training scale of teachers is about 3 million.
